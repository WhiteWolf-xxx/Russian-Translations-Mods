Oh yes, it is an invitation to the commissioning of Chasing Wind.

I remember this frivolity. Light shows. It illuminated the entire local group for many cycles.

What a strange decision for people who prided themselves on their austerity and spirituality.

Many made the trip despite this, coming from far and wide. But some simply refused to send any delegation.

It is hard to blame them. Such lavish festivities clearly show a total denigration of their roots.

That said, I remember feeling pleased at the thought of having an additional correspondent with whom to share my theories.
